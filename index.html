üìã BIR Form 2303 - Tax Registration Guide

When do you need to register?

  • When you earn ‚Ç±250,000+ per year from freelancing

  • Best practice: Register once you get your first client

What is Form 2303?

  • Certificate of Registration for self-employed/freelancers
  • Proves you're a legit business for tax purposes
  • Allows you to issue Official Receipts (OR)

How to register (Simple steps):

  • Step 1: Go to your RDO (Revenue District Office) based on your address
  • Step 2: Bring: Valid ID, Barangay Clearance, Cedula, Proof of Address
  • Step 3: Fill out BIR Form 1901 (for first-time registration)
  • Step 4: Pay ‚Ç±500 annual registration fee + documentary stamp
  • Step 5: Receive your TIN (Tax Identification Number) and Certificate
  • Step 6: Register books of accounts (‚Ç±200-300)

Tax obligations after registration:

  • Quarterly: File 1701Q (Percentage Tax) every 3 months - even if no income!
  • Annual: File 1701 (Income Tax Return) by April 15
  • 8% Percentage Tax: If earning below ‚Ç±3M/year (most freelancers)
  • OR Online filing: Use eBIRForms app or file in person

‚ö†Ô∏è Don't panic! Many beginners delay registration. Just register when you're ready - BIR penalties are negotiable for small freelancers.
